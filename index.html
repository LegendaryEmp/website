Getting Involved

The benefit of being a member of our Society is the opportunity to get involved in your Co-operative


As a business owned by our members we want you to be involved.


We want to be proud to trade in the heart of many communities, where we strive to provide both local activities and support for our members.


As a member, another way to involve yourself with your Co-operative is to attend meetings regularly and vote in the Society's decision elections, and by attending our Annual General Meeting.
